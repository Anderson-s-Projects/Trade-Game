HOW TO PLAY

GOAL: Survive 40 days and grow your net worth. End below $0 and you're BANKRUPT. Hit $50K to SURVIVE, $250K to become a TRADER, $1M for HEDGE FUND KING, $10M for MARKET LEGEND.

TRADING: Buy and sell assets in the TRADE tab. Prices differ by location — the same asset can be cheap in one place and expensive in another. Use this for geographic arbitrage: buy low, travel, sell high.

CAPACITY: Each asset takes up "capacity" per unit (crypto takes more). You have a limited trading capacity — manage your portfolio mix carefully.

TRAVEL: Moving locations advances a day and changes prices, volatility, and event types. Each location has its own bonus (shown under LOCATION).

STRESS: Rises from ending days, bad encounters, and shark visits. High stress (95%+) adds extra heat each day. Reduce it with a Vacation Day (Operations panel) — costs a day but resets stress.

HEAT: Rises from trading, loans, insider tips, and Dark Pool activity. High heat draws SEC investigations. Cool it down with a PR Firm (Operations panel) — costs cash, reduces heat.

REPUTATION: Builds from big profitable trades ($5K+) and repaying debt. Decays slowly each day. Higher rep unlocks cheaper loans, Hedge Fund access, and SEC leniency. Drop below 5 and Wall Street bans you.

THE SEC: Investigations trigger based on your behavior — heat, suspicious profits, Dark Pool trades, and insider tips. Pay the fine, hire a defense team, or try to flee offshore.

LOAN SHARK: If your debt gets too high, expect a visit every 7 days. Pay up, hide (risky), or borrow more (worse rates).

ENCOUNTERS: Random events — insider tips, hackers, influencer pumps, margin calls, IPOs, short reports. Each has real tradeoffs. Encounters stop in the final 2 days.

ALERTS: Set price alerts in the TRADE tab to get notified when an asset crosses a target price at your current location.
